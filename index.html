The Problems We Solve

Revenue teams don't have a dashboard problem. They have a trust problem.

Quarterdeck exists because the numbers leadership presents are only as good as the CRM underneath them.

Sales leaders don't trust their own forecast

Commit numbers are built on rep judgment and stale stage data. By the time the miss is visible, the quarter is over.

How Quarterdeck solves it

Every deal in the forecast gets a confidence score based on data freshness, activity, and stage history — so leaders see how much of the number is actually defensible.

CRM data decays silently

Close dates slip, owners change, activity stops — and nothing alerts anyone. Bad hygiene compounds until the pipeline is fiction.

How Quarterdeck solves it

Continuous hygiene monitoring flags stale, unowned, and inconsistent deals the moment they drift — with a prioritized fix list ordered by revenue impact.

Board prep takes days, not minutes

Ops teams spend hours every cycle reconciling exports, chasing reps, and hand-building slides that are outdated on arrival.

How Quarterdeck solves it

A Board Readiness Score plus auto-generated pipeline narrative turns board prep from a multi-day scramble into a review-and-send exercise.

Risk hides beneath the dashboard

Standard CRM reports show what reps entered — not what they stopped doing. Single-threaded and quietly dying deals look healthy until they vanish.

How Quarterdeck solves it

Deal-level risk flags surface stale, overdue, unowned, and single-threaded pipeline in seconds — before it costs you the quarter.

About KPAppWorx

A product company that builds like one.

Why we exist

Most AI projects die between the demo and the deployment. The prototype impresses everyone; the production system never arrives. KPAppWorx was founded on a simple observation: the gap isn't the models — it's the engineering discipline around them. We exist to close that gap.

Our mission

Build AI software that businesses can actually run on — measured not by how impressive it looks in a demo, but by whether it's still trusted and in use a year after launch.

How we build

We ship our own products — ten of them, live on the App Store, plus Quarterdeck AI — and we hold client work to the same bar. That means evaluation before excitement, read-only access before write access, boring reliable infrastructure over fashionable fragile infrastructure, and documentation treated as part of the product. Every system we deliver is one we'd be willing to operate ourselves. Usually, we do.

Where we're going

Quarterdeck AI is the first of a line of focused, deeply-built AI products for revenue and operations teams. The product side funds our independence; the client side keeps us sharp against real-world constraints. Both make each other better.
